Heatmap & Vector Visualization Platform

A multi-page pathology platform for exploring Whole-Slide Images (WSI) with AI-powered analysis.

  • Upload Portal: Upload and manage WSI files with automatic metadata extraction.
  • Slide Viewer: View slides with real-time color deconvolution (H&E, DAB channels).
  • Analyze: AI model selection with heatmap overlays and vector flow visualization.
